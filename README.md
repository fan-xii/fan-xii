## 🌱 About Me

Hey there! I'm a developer who loves tinkering with AI, building desktop applications, and exploring new technologies. I believe in learning through hands-on projects — from LLM integrations and prompt engineering to WPF desktop apps and data visualization tools.

Currently diving deep into AI agents, LLM workflows, and how to make machines think (or at least pretend to). When I'm not coding, you'll find me experimenting with new frameworks and shipping side projects.
